AN OVERVIEW OF BLOOD TRANSFUSION OVER THE YEARS

January 12,2021 at 12.00pm
By Dr.Shihara | Manager

Blood has been utilized as a treatment for a number of diseas since
the 17th century.There have been numerous significant advancements
throughout the years, so it's no suprise that this rich resource is in
such high demand. Here are some of the more significant milestones in
the history of blood transfusion.
Commentaries


"Arogya Parama Labha" award of apperciation in
2021 Swarna Wibhushana for providing a
great service to patients who are in need of blood.
Commedations


2019 Mar : The first recorded successful blood transfusion occurs in NDBS Sri Lanka.
2020 Jan : The first HIV blood test has been approved and is being used by blood banks
2020 Sep : First blood donation Campaign was held successfully.
2021 Jun : A specific test to identify Covid-19 is introduced.
